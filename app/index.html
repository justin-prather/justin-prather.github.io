At Bradon Equestrian we are committed to the development of horse and rider in the jumper ring. The facility is owned and operated by Brad and Valerie Prather. It was developed out of a desire by the Prather Family to train on the west side of Calgary in a first class facility with quality training and horse care. Through their children, the Prather’s have been involved in show jumping from the lead line to the Grand Prix ring for over a decade. They also own horses that have competed at the international level in Canada, the United States and Europe with Jill Henselwood, a member of the Canadian Equestrian Team.

As parents and owners, the Prathers understand the investment and commitment required to compete in the show jumping world. With this unique perspective, the Prathers offer boarding and training at their facility and are committed to providing members of Bradon Equestrian with the same level of training, facilities, equine care, and transparency in the purchase and sale of horses that they have come to expect for themselves.

Bradon Paramedical Services Ltd. was established to provide leading edge, advanced life support care to Alberta employers who require private paramedic services. Our lead paramedics have over 40 years of combined experience in providing emergency medical services. These paramedics joined the Bradon Group of Companies in order to continue to offer their expertise to a wide variety of industries with the support of a long standing Alberta owned company with significant experience in the construction, oil and gas servicing and equestrian industries. The Bradon Group of companies know, from experience, what employers want and need in safety services and Bradon Paramedical is ideally positioned to provide those services in a professional, client-oriented manner.

Bradon Paramedical provides 24/7 advanced life support care with experienced paramedics who are registered with the Alberta College of Paramedics and are active practitioners in the pre-hospital setting. This expertise is provided to a wide variety of industries including, commercial, construction, oil and gas, government and equestrian.

We look forward to providing your company with the paramedical services required to meet or exceed government standards and to provide a safe environment for your clients.
